1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners useful information about how their site is used.

This Cookie Policy explains how SmartVibes Limited ("SmartVibes", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on smartvibes.tech and what choices you have.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential functionality — to ensure our website works correctly and securely
  • Analytics — to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (e.g. which pages are most visited, where visitors come from)
  • Performance — to ensure pages load quickly and efficiently
  • Preferences — to remember your settings and choices (such as cookie consent)

We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites. Our website does not display third-party advertisements.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our site by collecting anonymous usage data. No personal information is tracked.

Required if you view embedded video content (YouTube, Vimeo). These platforms may set their own cookies when media loads.

5. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

In addition to the controls above, you can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies have been set and delete them
  • Block all cookies from specific websites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For guidance on managing cookies in your specific browser, visit your browser's help documentation.
